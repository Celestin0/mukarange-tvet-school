Courses

Mukarange-TVET-School have Three special combinations and Shortcourses you can Choose one of among those combinations.

Masonary

Masonary(MAS) is one of the two academic options within Mukarange-tvet-shool. The option has excellent laboratories, workshops and state of art computing facilities. Masonry option equip students with skills and knowledge at Higher level diploma in Construction Technology, besides it offers a big range of short courses mainly in Painting. With a target of meeting the labour market needs, Masonry provides high quality engineering education that meets the requirement for a professional practitioner in Civil Engineering sector.
